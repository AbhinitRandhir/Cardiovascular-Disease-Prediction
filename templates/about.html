About Cardiovascular Disease Prediction

Cardiovascular Disease Prediction is a cutting-edge diagnostic tool designed to bridge the gap between complex medical data and personal health awareness. Our platform uses Random Forest Machine Learning models trained on clinical datasets to predict the probability of cardiovascular issues based on 13 key physiological factors.

The Technology

We utilize robust data scaling and ensemble learning techniques to ensure high accuracy. By analyzing variables like cholesterol, heart rate, and ST-segment depression, we provide a data-backed risk score.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower users with early-detection capabilities. While this is not a replacement for a doctor, it serves as a powerful first-step indicator for potential health concerns.

Developed using Flask, Scikit-Learn, and Modern Web Standards.
